# 格林公式的若干形式
$
\begin{aligned}
&设\Omega 是Guass公式中的闭区域,\\
&u,v\in C^2(\Omega),\vec{n}表示\partial \Omega 的单位外法向量\\
&\\
&则我们有下述结论成立:\\
&(1)格林恒等式:\int_{\partial \Omega}\frac{\partial u}{\partial \vec{n}}d\sigma =\int _{\Omega}\Delta ud\mu \\
&(2)格林第一恒等式:\int_{\partial \Omega}v\frac{\partial u}{\partial \vec{n}}d\sigma=\int_{\Omega}\nabla  u\cdot \nabla vd\mu +\int_{\Omega}v\Delta ud\mu \\
&(3)格林第二恒等式:\int_{\partial \Omega}(v\frac{\partial u}{\partial \vec{n}}-u\frac{\partial v}{\partial \vec{n}})d\sigma =\int_{\Omega} (v\Delta u-u\Delta v)d\mu \\
&\\\\
&证明:\\
&(1)\int_{\partial \Omega}\frac{\partial u}{\partial \vec{n}}d\sigma =\int_{\partial \Omega}\nabla u\cdot \vec{n}d\sigma \\
&=\int_{\Omega}\nabla \cdot \nabla ud\mu\\
&=\int_{\Omega}\Delta ud\mu \\
&\\\\
&(2)\int_{\partial \Omega}v\frac{\partial u}{\partial \vec{n}}d\sigma=\int_{\partial \Omega}v\nabla u\cdot \vec{n}d\sigma\\
&=\int_{\Omega}\nabla \cdot (v\nabla u)d\mu \\
&=\int_{\Omega}\nabla v\cdot \nabla ud\mu +\int_{\Omega}v\Delta ud\mu \\
&\\\\
&(3)应用(2)的结论我们有\\
&\int_{\partial \Omega}(v\frac{\partial u}{\partial \vec{n}}-u\frac{\partial v}{\partial \vec{n}})d\sigma\\&=\int_{\Omega}\nabla v\cdot \nabla ud\mu +\int_{\Omega}v\Delta ud\mu -\int_{\Omega}\nabla v\cdot \nabla ud\mu +\int_{\Omega}v\Delta ud\mu \\
&=\int_{\Omega} (v\Delta u-u\Delta v)d\mu \\
\end{aligned}
$
## 应用
$
\begin{aligned}
&设f,g均在区域\Omega\subset R^2上有二阶连续偏导数,\\
&满足\Delta g=0\\
&f|_{\partial \Omega}=g|_{\partial \Omega},证明:\\
&\int_{\Omega}\nabla f\cdot \nabla f\geq \int_{\Omega}\nabla g\cdot \nabla g\\
&我们直接应用第一Green公式\\
&\int_{\partial \Omega }f\frac{\partial g}{\partial \vec{n}}d\sigma =\int_{\Omega}\nabla f\cdot \nabla gd\mu+\int_{\Omega}f\Delta gd\mu \\
&\int_{\partial \Omega}g\frac{\partial f}{\partial \vec{n}}d\sigma =\int_{\Omega}\nabla g\cdot \nabla fd\mu+\int_{\Omega}g\Delta fd\mu \\
&\Rightarrow \int_{\Omega}f\Delta gd\mu =\int_{\Omega}g\Delta fd\mu \\
&故知\int_{\Omega}\nabla f\cdot \nabla f-\nabla g\cdot \nabla gd\mu=\int_{\Omega}(\nabla f-\nabla g)^2d\mu\geq 0\\
&故知原命题成立\\
\end{aligned}
$
