# 变分法
## 前言
本文的变分法只应用于一系列积分不等式问题的猜答案工作
不进行最值具体存在性的讨论
## 泛函数(Functionals)
泛函数就是函数的函数,在变分法中,泛函通常是一个积分
$I=\int_{x_1}^{x_2}Fdx$
## 一阶泛函
$
\begin{aligned}
&记I=\int_{x_1}^{x_2}F(y,y',x)dx\\
&我们可以引入一个微小量\epsilon 和一个任意一个可微函数\eta(x)表示\tilde{y}:\\
&\tilde{y}(x)=y(x)+\epsilon \eta (x)\\
&把I(\epsilon)关于\epsilon 做taylor展开\\
&其中的一次项被称为一阶变分,记为\delta I\\
&二阶变分为Taylor展开中的二次项,记为\delta I^2\\
&令\delta I=0,即可得到可以使I取得极值的y\\
&我们接下来进行具体计算\\
&I(\epsilon)=\int_{x_1}^{x_2}F(\tilde{y},\tilde{y}',x)dx\\
&\delta I=\int_{x_1}^{x_2}(\frac{\partial F}{\partial \tilde{y}}\frac{\partial \tilde{y}}{\partial \epsilon}+\frac{\partial F}{\partial \tilde{y}'}\frac{\partial \tilde{y}'}{\partial \epsilon})\epsilon dx\\
&而由\tilde{y}=y+\epsilon\eta\\
&故有\delta \tilde{y}=\eta ,\delta \tilde{y}'=\eta '\\
&\Rightarrow \delta I=\int_{x_1}^{x_2}(\frac{\partial F}{\partial y}\eta+\frac{\partial F}{\partial y'}\eta ')\epsilon dx=0\\
&即\int_{x_1}^{x_2}\frac{\partial F}{\partial y}\eta+\frac{\partial F}{\partial y'}\eta' dx=0\\
&\int_{x_1}^{x_2}\frac{\partial F}{\partial y'}\eta 'dx=\int_{x_1}^{x_2}\frac{\partial F}{\partial y'}d\eta\\
&使用分部积分\\
&=\frac{\partial F}{\partial y'}\eta _{x_1}^{x_2}-\int_{x_1}^{x_2}\eta \frac{d}{dx}\frac{\partial F}{\partial y'}dx\\
&故由\eta 的边界值为0\\
&且由\eta 的任意性可知\\
&\frac{\partial F}{\partial y}-\frac{d}{dx}\frac{\partial F}{\partial y'}=0\\
&上式即为所谓的Euler-Lagrange方程\\
\end{aligned}
$
## 二阶泛函
$
\begin{aligned}
&若F为关于y,y',y'',x的函数\\
&我们下面继续考虑所求泛函的最值\\
&\tilde{I}=\int_{x_1}^{x_2}F(\tilde{y},\tilde{y}',\tilde{y}'',x)dx\\
&其中\tilde{y}=y+\epsilon \eta\\
&则\delta \tilde{I} =\int_{x_1}^{x_2}\frac{\partial F}{\partial \epsilon }\epsilon dx\\
&\frac{\partial F}{\partial \epsilon}=\frac{\partial F}{\partial \tilde{y}}\frac{\partial \tilde{y}}{\partial \epsilon}+\frac{\partial F}{\partial \tilde{y}'}\frac{\partial \tilde{y}'}{\partial \epsilon}+\frac{\partial F}{\partial \tilde{y}''}\frac{\partial \tilde{y}''}{\partial \epsilon}\\
&故知\tilde{I}=\int_{x_1}^{x_2}\frac{\partial F}{\partial y}\eta+\frac{\partial F}{\partial y'}\eta '+\frac{\partial F}{\partial y''}\eta ''dx\\
&我们仍然是进行分部积分\\
&\int_{x_1}^{x_2}\frac{\partial F}{\partial y''}\eta ''dx=\int_{x_1}^{x_2}\frac{\partial F}{\partial y''}d\eta '\\
&=\frac{\partial F}{\partial y''}\eta '|_{x_1}^{x_2}-\int_{x_1}^{x_2}\frac{d}{dx}\frac{\partial F}{\partial y''}d\eta\\
&=-\int_{x_1}^{x_2}\frac{d}{dx}\frac{\partial F}{\partial y''}d\eta\\
&=\int_{x_1}^{x_2}\eta \frac{d^2}{dx^2}\frac{\partial F}{\partial y''}dx\\
&故知\frac{\partial F}{\partial y}-\frac{d}{dx}\frac{\partial F}{\partial y'}+\frac{d^2}{dx^2}\frac{\partial F}{\partial y''}=0\\
&此即为二阶泛函的Euler-Lagrange方程\\
\end{aligned}
$
## 带约束条件的变分法
$
\begin{aligned}
&上面介绍的变分法是在没有约束条件的情况下进行的\\
&事实上对于有约束条件的变分法\\
&我们可以仿照Lagrange乘数法进行求解\\
&即我们不妨设边界条件为\\
&J(y)=\int_{x_1}^{x_2}G(y,y',x)dx=constant\\
&则若我们要求解I在该条件下的极值\\
&则我们令新泛函I^*=I+\lambda J\\
&我们令\delta I^*=0\\
&结合边界条件所解出的函数即是I取极值的函数\\
\end{aligned}
$
## 应用
$
\begin{aligned}
&利用变分法,我们可以证明几乎所有的积分不等式\\
&其中最经典的应用是证明Wirtinger不等式\\
&(Wirtinger不等式)设f(x)是[-\pi,\pi]上的连续可微函数\\
&且f(-\pi)=f(\pi),若\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}f(x)dx=0\\
&求证:\\
&\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}f^2(x)dx\leq\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}f'(x)^2dx\\
&I=\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}f^2(x)-f'(x)^2dx\\
&i.e. I=\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}F(f,f',x)dx\\
&其中F=f^2(x)-f'(x)^2dx\\
&则由Euler-Lagrange方程可知\\
&\frac{\partial F}{\partial f}-\frac{d }{dx}\frac{\partial F}{\partial f'}=0\\
&\Rightarrow 2f(x)+\frac{d}{dx}2f'(x)=0\\
&i.e. f(x)+f''(x)=0\\
&\Rightarrow f(x)=asinx+bcosx\\
&带入验证可知此时I=0\\
&故知原命题成立\\
\end{aligned}
$
