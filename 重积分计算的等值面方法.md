# 重积分计算的等值面(线)方法
## 等值面(线)方法
$
\begin{aligned}
&二重积分:\\
&设函数f(x,y)的等位线是简单封闭曲线,\\
&D(t_1,t_2)是由曲线f(x,y)=t_1,f(x,y)=t_2所围成的区域\\
&f(x,y)在D(t_1,t_2)上连续,\\
&F(t)是由等位线f(x,y)=t_1,f(x,y)=t_2所包围的区域的面积\\
&h(t),F'(t)在[t_1,t_2]中存在且可积,\\
&则\iint _{D(t_1,t_2)}h(f(x,y))dxdy=\int_{t_1}^{t_2}h(t)dF(t)\\
\\\\
&对于三重积分可同理类比:\\
&设函数f(x,y,z)的等位线是简单封闭曲线,\\
&D(t_1,t_2)是由曲面f(x,y,z)=t_1与f(x,y,z)=t_2所围成的区域\\
&f(x,y,z)在D(t_1,t_2)上连续,\\
&F(t)是由等位线f(x,y,z)=t_1,f(x,y,z)=t_2所包围部分的体积\\
&h(t),F'(t)在[t_1,t_2]中存在且可积,则\\
&\iiint_{D(t_1,t_2)}h(f(x,y,z))dxdydz=\int_{t_1}^{t_2}h(t)dF(t)\\
&\\\\
&特别地:\\
&我们可以推导出单位圆和单位球上的重积分的等值面计算公式\\
&\iint_{D}f(x,y)dxdy=\int_{0}^{1}dr\int_{x^2+y^2=r^2}f(x,y)ds\\
&(其中D:x^2+y^2\leq 1)\\
&\\\\
&\iiint_{D}f(x,y,z)dxdydz=\int_{0}^{1}dr\int_{x^2+y^2+z^2=r^2}f(x,y,z)d\sigma \\
&其中D:x^2+y^2+z^2\leq 1\\
&\\\\
&对于柱体也有类似的结果(应用可见例(3))\\
&\iiint_{D}f(x,y,z)dxdydz=\int_{0}^{1}dr\int _{|x|+|y|+|z|=r}f(x,y,z)d\sigma \\
&其中D:|x|+|y|+|z|\leq 1\\
&\\\\
&通过上述例子我们不难发现:\\
&等值面法其实是对被积区域进行划分的一种方式\\
&其本质是将重积分转化成累次积分\\
\end{aligned}
$
## 应用
$
\begin{aligned}
&例(1)计算\iint_{D}(x^2+y^2)dxdy\\
&其中D:x^2+y^2\leq 1\\
&\iint _{D}(x^2+y^2)dxdy=\int_{0}^{1}r2\pi r dr=\frac{\pi }{2}\\
&\\\\
&例(2)计算\iiint_{D}(x^2+y^2+z^2)dxdydz\\
&其中D:x^2+y^2+z^2\leq 1\\
&\iiint_{D}(x^2+y^2+z^2)dxdydz=\int_{0}^{1}drr^24\pi r^2=\frac{4\pi }{5}\\
&\\\\
&例(3)计算\iiint_{\Omega}\frac{dxdydz}{(1+|x|+|y|+|z|)^3}\\
&其中\Omega :|x|+|y|+|z|\leq 1\\
&\iiint_{\Omega}\frac{dxdydz}{(1+|x|+|y|+|z|)^3}=\int_{0}^{1}dr\iint_{|x|+|y|+|z|=r}\frac{d\sigma }{(1+|x|+|y|+|z|)^3}\\
&=4\int_{0}^{1}\frac{r^2}{(1+r)^3}dr=4ln2-\frac{5}{2}\\\\\\
&例(4)设函数f(x,y)在闭区域D:x^2+y^2\leq 1上有二阶连续偏导数\\
&且\frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2}+\frac{\partial ^2 f}{\partial y^2}=e^{-(x^2+y^2)}\\
&证明:\iint_{D}(x\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}+y\frac{\partial f}{\partial y})dxdy=\frac{\pi }{2e}\\
&\iint_{D}(x\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}+y\frac{\partial f}{\partial y})dxdy=\int_{0}^{1}dr\int_{x^2+y^2=r^2}(x\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}+y\frac{\partial f}{\partial y})ds\\
&=\int_{0}^{1}dr\int_{x^2+y^2=r^2}(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial f}{\partial y})\cdot (x,y)ds\\
&=\int_{0}^{1}rdr\int _{x^2+y^2\leq r^2}\nabla \cdot (\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial f}{\partial y})d\sigma\\
&=\int_{0}^{1}rdr\int _{x^2+y^2\leq r^2}(\frac{\partial ^2f}{\partial x^2}+\frac{\partial ^2f}{\partial y^2})d\sigma\\
&=\int_{0}^{1}rdr\int _{x^2+y^2\leq r^2}e^{-(x^2+y^2)}d\sigma\\
&\int _{x^2+y^2\leq r^2}e^{-(x^2+y^2)}d\sigma=\int_{0}^{r}\int_{0}^{2\pi}e^{-r^2}rdr\\
&=\pi (1-e^{-r^2})\\
&\\
&\int_{0}^{1}dr\int _{x^2+y^2=r^2}e^{-(x^2+y^2)}ds\\
&=\int _{0}^{1}\pi r(1-e^{-r^2})dr\\
&=\pi (1-(1-e^{-1}))\frac{1}{2}\\
&=\frac{\pi }{2e}\\
&例(4)实质上是与重积分分部积分法一文中的两道例题同类的题型\\
&事实上用等值面(线)方法也可以解决分部积分法一文中的两道题目\\
&具体实现与本文例(4)同理，在此不再赘述\\
\end{aligned}
$
