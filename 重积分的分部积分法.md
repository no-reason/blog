# 重积分的分部积分法
## 二三重积分的分部积分公式
$
\begin{aligned}
&二重积分的分部积分公式:\\
&\iint_{D} u\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}dxdy=\int_{\partial D}uvdy-\iint_D v\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}dxdy\\
&\iint_{D}u\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}dxdy=-\int_{\partial D} uvdx-\iint_{D}v\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}dxdy\\
&\\\\
&三重积分的分部积分公式:\\
&\iiint_{D}u\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}dxdydz=\oiint_{\partial D}uvdydz-\iiint_{D}v\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}dxdydz\\
&(对于y,z而言完全对称)\\
&\\\\
\end{aligned}
$
## 应用
$
\begin{aligned}
&(1)(CMC非数9决)\\
&设f(x,y,z)在单位球区域内有连续二阶偏导数\\
&且有\frac{\partial ^2 f}{\partial x^2}+\frac{\partial ^2 f}{\partial y^2}+\frac{\partial ^2f}{\partial z^2}=\sqrt{x^2+y^2+z^2}\\
&求I=\iiint_{x^2+y^2+z^2\leq 1}(x\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}+y\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial f}{\partial z})dxdydz\\
&利用三重积分的分部积分公式\\
&I=\oiint_{x+2+y^2+z^2=1}(\frac{(x^2+y^2+z^2)}{2}\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}dydz+\frac{(x^2+y^2+z^2)}{2}\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}dzdx+\frac{(x^2+y^2+z^2)}{2}\frac{\partial f}{\partial z}dxdy)\\
&-\iiint_{x^2+y^2+z^2\leq 1}\frac{x^2+y^2+z^2}{2}(\frac{\partial ^2 f}{\partial x^2}+\frac{\partial ^2 f}{\partial y^2}+\frac{\partial ^2f}{\partial z^2})dxdydz\\
&=\iiint_{x^2+y^2+z^2\leq 1}[\frac{1}{2}(\frac{\partial ^2 f}{\partial x^2}+\frac{\partial ^2 f}{\partial y^2}+\frac{\partial ^2f}{\partial z^2})-\frac{1}{2}(x^2+y^2+z^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}]dxdydz\\
&=\iiint_{x^2+y^2+z^2\leq 1}\frac{(x^2+y^2+z^2)^{\frac{1}{2}}-(x^2+y^2+z^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}}{2}dxdydz\\
&=\frac{\pi}{6}\\
&\\\\
&(2)设f(x,y)在区域D=\{(x,y)|x^2+y^2\leq a^2\}上具有一阶连续偏导数\\
&且满足f(x,y)|_{x^2+y^2=a^2}=a^2,(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x})^2+(\frac{\partial f}{\partial y})^2\leq a^2(\forall (x,y)\in D).\\
&证明:|\iint_D f(x,y)dxdy|\leq \frac{4}{3}\pi a^4\\
&应用分部积分法可知\\
&\iint_{D}f(x,y)dxdy=\int_{\partial D}xfdy-\iint _{D}x\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}dxdy\\
&\iint_{D}f(x,y)dxdy=-\int_{\partial D}yfdx-\iint _{D}y\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}dxdy\\
&故知\\
&2\iint_{D}f(x,y)dxdy=\int_{\partial D}xfdy-yfdx-\iint_{D}x\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}+y\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}dxdy\\
&\int_{\partial D}xfdy-yfdx=a^2\int_{\partial D}xdy-ydx\\
&=a^2\cdot 2\cdot \pi a^2\\
&=2\pi a^4\\
&\iint_{D}x\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}+y\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}dxdy\leq \iint_{D}\sqrt{x^2+y^2}\sqrt{(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x})^2+(\frac{\partial f}{\partial y})^2}dxdy\\
&\leq a\iint_{D}\sqrt{x^2+y^2}dxdy\\
&=\frac{2\pi a^4}{3}\\
&故知所求式\leq \frac{4}{3}\pi a^4\\
\\\\
\end{aligned}
$
