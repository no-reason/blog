&(1)有界闭区间上的连续函数一定一致连续\\
&设函数f在[a,b]上连续，则f在[a,b]上一定一致连续\\
证明：&\\
&法一(有限覆盖定理)：\\
&\forall x_0 \in [a,b]，由f(x)连续知，\exists B_{\delta _{0}}(x_0)\\
&s.t. \forall x \in B_{\delta _{0}}(x_0)  有|f(x)-f(x_0)|<\epsilon\\
&而[a,b]\subseteq \cup _{x_0\in [a,b]}B_{\delta _{0}}(x_0)\\
&而由有限覆盖定理知\exists 1\leq i \leq n\\
&s.t. [a,b]\subseteq \cup _{1\leq i \leq n}B_{\delta _{i}}(x_i)\\
&我们取\delta=\frac{1}{2}min_{1\leq i\leq n}\delta_{i}\\
&下证\forall |y_1-y_2|<\delta 有|f(y_1)-f(y_2)|<\epsilon\\
&我们不妨设y_1\in (x_i,x_{i+1}),y_1<y_2\\
&则|x_{i+1}-y_1|<\epsilon ,|x_{i+1}-y_2|<\epsilon\\
&故|y_1-y_2|\leq|x_{i+1}-y_1|+|x_{i+1}-y_2|<2\epsilon\\
&故有f(x)一致连续\\
